# Startup Landscape in Turkiye

The fun project I started to understand Turkish tech scene how media represents has revealed a lot of interesting points I found worth to share.

-   Acquisition deal sizes are smaller than the late.
-   Turkiye lacks late stage investment culture.
-   Most late stage deals involve foreign investors.

## Motivation

-   Avoiding media for startup news.
-   Retain visualization skills.

## Notes on the work

-   Covers [ 2020 Q1, 2024 Q4 ].
-   Excludes deals with undisclosed details.
-   Based on [212 and KPMG Turkish Startup Investment Reviews](https://212.vc/turkish-startup-investment-reviews/?utm_source=ufukty.com).
-   Work in progress. Iterating over presentation.
-   Codebase is [ufukty/landscape](https://github.com/ufukty/landscape?utm_source=ufukty.com).
-   <a href="mailto:ufukty@gmail.com">Report</a> data-claim disconnect.
